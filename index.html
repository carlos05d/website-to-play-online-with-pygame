Welcome to Sniper Game!

Sniper Game is an exciting arcade-style game where you control a sniper to shoot down falling enemies. Test your reflexes and see how high you can score!

Instructions:
- Use the left and right arrow keys to move.
- Press the spacebar to shoot.
- Avoid missing enemies to keep your lives!
